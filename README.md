# Hot Companies Job Board

A curated job board that ONLY shows jobs from hot, funded companies (Seed, Series A/B/C/D, Pre-IPO, Public Companies). We scrape jobs directly from company career pages - no 3rd party postings. AI matches developers to these high-quality opportunities.
